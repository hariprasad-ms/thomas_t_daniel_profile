I am Thomas T. Daniel, Ph.D. in Nanotechnology from IIT Guwahati, specializing in device fabrication for sensing applications. Co-founder of K-FAB Tech Private Limited, I aim to make semiconductor fabrication equipment accessible to researchers. My experience includes managing the cleanroom at the India Innovation Centre for Graphene (IICG) and consulting on micro and nanofabrication facility setups.

ACADEMIC QUALIFICATION



  • Doctor of Philosophy in Nanotechnology, IIT Guwahati

    December 2021

    I completed my PhD in Nanotechnology at IIT Guwahati, Assam, India. My thesis, titled "Magnetic and Semiconductor Devices for the Analysis of Breath Components," focused on the design, fabrication, testing, and modeling of transistors, diodes, sensors, and magnetic devices. I was guided by Prof. Roy Paily Palathinkaly throughout my doctoral research.

  • Master of Engineering in Power Electronics and Drives, Anna University

    May 2013

    I earned my Master of Engineering in Power Electronics and Drives from Anna University, Chennai, India. My studies were centered on power quality, where I gained in-depth knowledge and expertise in managing and improving electrical power systems.

  • Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management, University of Kerala

    November 2011

    I hold a Post Graduate Diploma in Human Resource Management from the University of Kerala, Kerala, India. This program focused on a strategic approach to human resource management, equipping me with skills in strategic planning and personnel management.

  • Bachelor of Engineering Electrical and Electronics, Sathyabama University.

    March 2010

    I completed my Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical and Electronics at Sathyabama University. My undergraduate education provided me with a solid foundation in electrical systems and electronic circuits, setting the stage for my advanced studies and research.
